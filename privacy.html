Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

Sofia Content Manager ("the App") is a private automation tool used to publish video content to TikTok on behalf of authorized accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data.

Data We Collect

The App collects only the following data, obtained through TikTok's OAuth authorization flow:

  • TikTok User ID (open_id) — used solely to identify the authorized account for posting
  • TikTok access token and refresh token — stored locally and used only to authenticate API requests

We do not collect names, email addresses, profile photos, follower data, or any other personal information.

How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively to publish video content to the authorized TikTok account. It is never shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising or analytics purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the operator's device. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond what is required to communicate with TikTok's API.

Third-Party Services

The App communicates only with TikTok's official API (open.tiktokapis.com). No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
